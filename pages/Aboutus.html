Welcome to Our Website

We are Year - 8 Indigo students from EDUSN Digital school.

Our Project's Timeline

April 23 - May 8
Recycled art exhibition

Our team held a Recycled Art Exhibition at our school, showcasing creative artworks made from reused materials like cardboard, plastic bottles, and tin foil. The event featured sculptures, models, and presentations about the importance of recycling. The exhibition was well-attended by teachers, students, and parents, and we felt proud of our efforts to turn waste into art and raise awareness about protecting the environment.

May 9 - May 13
Visting recycling education centres

We visited a Recycling Education Centre, where we learned how waste is sorted and reused through machines and systems. We took part in interactive games and watched a video about the dangers of not recycling. The visit helped us students understand the importance of recycling and encouraged us to take action at home and school to help protect the environment.

May 14 - May 20
Recycling Brainstorm Day

We took part in a brainstorming activity to come up with creative ways to recycle. We shared ideas like reusing jars, repurposing cardboard, swapping items instead of throwing them away, and setting up recycling stations at school. The session encouraged teamwork and showed how small, creative actions can help protect the environment.

May 21 - May 27
Decorate your recycling bins

Our team participated in a fun activity where we decorated recycling bins to promote better recycling habits. Each of us designed a bin for a specific material like paper, plastic, or metal, using bright colours, drawings, and catchy slogans. The decorated bins helped make recycling more engaging for us and reminded us to sort our waste correctly. The activity encouraged creativity and raised awareness about environmental responsibility.

Our Purpose

The purpose of our project is to create an informative and engaging website that raises awareness about recycling. Our goal is to educate people on the importance of recycling, provide practical tips on how to recycle effectively, and encourage people in our community to help protect our environment for ongoing generations.

Our vision for this website

Recycling reduces landfill waste, conserves natural resources, and lowers pollution. It’s a simple habit that leads to big environmental benefits. Every can, bottle, or paper you recycle helps protect the planet for future generations.
